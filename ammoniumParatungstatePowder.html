Description

Ammonium Paratungstate popularly known as APT is a white crystalline powder. It is used as an intermediate product for manufacturing of tungsten trioxide, tungsten metal and its derivatives.

    Applications:
  • Fireproofing of fabric and cellulose
  • Reagent for uric acid
  • Clinical analysis of blood
  • Textile plastics
  • Yellow pigment in ceramic
  • Organometallic compounds
  • Precursor for catalyst
