Consultancy

Fonsmet Materials Pvt Ltd is headed by highly educated, experienced, and skilled technocrats in the field of metallurgy, materials science, and chemical engineering. Being a Research and Development driven organization, we feel responsible to develop cost-effective and greener technologies resulting in high purity materials. In this world of constant technological developments, the need for high tech application metals & compounds is becoming crucial. We aim to develop and provide our clients with new technologies to stand out in the world's competition and be a leader.

“Let’s join our hands together for a better tomorrow”

Process Consultancy

  • Recycling of Li-ion batteries and recovering technical/battery grade Lithium compounds, Cobalt and nickel salts for electroplating and battery applications.
  • Lithium extraction and purification from its mineral/ore.
  • Cobalt and Nickel extraction and purification from its mineral/ore/scrap via solvent extraction.
  • Zirconium & Hafnium separation for nuclear and pharmaceutical grade Zirconium compounds (Hf < 100 ppm) via solvent extraction.
  • Molybdenum & tungsten extraction and purification from Ores or catalyst scrap via solvent extraction.
  • Chrome extraction from its mineral and scrap.
  • Titanium extraction and purification from scrap and mineral.
  • Master alloy manufacturing.

Our Approach

  • In-depth discussion with clients regarding the variety of raw materials to be processed and land available with a view to develop processes with capability to process variety of raw materials.
  • 3 lab trials to establish consistency.
  • 3 pilot plant trials to establish consistency and identify any problems in scale-up.
  • Basic PFD, P&ID, and layout design.
  • Assistance for MOC selections and technical discussions with the vendors.
  • Heat and material balance.
  • Utility consumption.
  • Plant Commissioning.
  • Provide technical team and deputation at the plant to take care of the smooth running of operations and ensure consistent quality of the output products.
